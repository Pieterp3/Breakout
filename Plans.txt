Breakout

1. Blocks come down from top  of screen
2. Player is a paddle on bottom of screen
3. A ball starts on the players paddle
4. Round starts when player presses space
5. Ball bounces off of walls and breaks blocks
6. Round lost when ball hits underneath players paddle
7. Points given for breaking bricks
